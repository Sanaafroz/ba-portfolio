## ✅ Acceptance Criteria Template
Given [context/precondition]
When [action is taken]
Then [expected outcome]
**Example for US-001:**
Given the customer is on the account opening page
When they fill all required details and submit
Then the system creates an account and sends
a confirmation email within 2 minutes
